Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

TL;DR: Vessel does not collect any personal data. Everything stays on your device. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no ads. Ever.

1. Data We Collect

None. Vessel does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on remote servers. We have no user accounts, no login, and no backend infrastructure.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

All data you enter into Vessel is stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's SwiftData framework:

  • Logged drinks (type, amount, timestamp, optional notes)
  • Settings (daily goal, preferred unit, reminder schedule, theme, body weight)
  • Quick-add presets

This data never leaves your device. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed.

3. Apple Health (HealthKit)

Vessel offers optional integration with Apple Health. If you enable this in Settings:

  • When you log a drink, water and caffeine amounts are written to HealthKit on your device
  • Apple Health data stays on your device and is encrypted by iOS
  • You can revoke permission any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health

Vessel never reads HealthKit data for any purpose other than displaying your own intake within the app.

4. Notifications

If you enable hydration reminders, Vessel uses Apple's local notification system to send you reminders on your chosen schedule. No notification data is sent to our servers because we don't have any servers.

5. Third-Party Services

Vessel contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking services of any kind. The app makes no network requests except for Apple StoreKit purchase and subscription entitlement checks, and periodic App Store version checks — none of which contain any personal data.

6. In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

Vessel offers an optional Vessel Pro subscription. Purchases and subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit:

  • Payment is handled by Apple; we never see or store your payment details (card numbers, billing address, etc.)
  • We receive only anonymized transaction records from Apple to verify your subscription entitlement
  • You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions

7. Children's Privacy

Vessel is not directed at children under 13. Because we collect no data, no child data could ever be collected or processed.

8. Your Rights

Since we collect no data, there is nothing for us to export, delete, or correct on our end. All control over your data lives in your device:

  • To delete all drink data: Settings → Data → "Delete all drink data"
  • To revoke HealthKit access: iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Vessel
  • To delete everything: Delete the app from your iPhone

9. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how the app handles data, we'll update this policy and note the change at the top.
